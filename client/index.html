URL Shortener

To get a shortened URL, add /new/ to the url, followed by your full website.

To be redirected to your full URL, browse to the shortened url you were given.

Example

https://fcc-basejump-url-duckydisciple.c9users.io/new/http://www.google.com
https://fcc-basejump-url-duckydisciple.c9users.io/0
